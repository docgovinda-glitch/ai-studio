# Vision

AI Studio is an AI Operating System that unifies AI-powered creation, research, automation, and publishing into one extensible platform.

Rather than forcing users to switch between multiple AI tools, AI Studio enables them to complete their entire workflow from a single application.

---

# Mission

To build the world's most comprehensive AI platform for creators, researchers, educators, businesses, and organizations.

---

# Core Principles

## AI First

Every workflow should intelligently leverage AI.

---

## Human First

---

## Modular

---

## Extensible

AI Studio should support plugins, AI agents, and workflows.

Become the operating system for AI-assisted knowledge creation and content production.

Users should be able to complete an entire project without leaving AI Studio.
